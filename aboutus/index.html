ABOUT US

Driven by excellence, we design spaces that expand what’s possible and create lasting value through thoughtful, buildable solutions.

Welcome to VISIO Architecture, where innovative design meets timeless functionality. We are passionate about creating inspiring spaces and crafting bespoke architectural solutions for residential, commercial, and urban projects. Our team brings ideas to life by blending creativity, sustainability, and modern technology. From concept design to project management, we are committed to delivering outcomes that exceed expectations while remaining in harmony with their surroundings. At VISIO, our mission is to design and deliver innovative, sustainable, and functional solutions that turn vision into reality. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and shape spaces that inspire and endure—enhancing the built environment, improving community life, and leaving a lasting architectural legacy.
Gaetan SIEW and Vinesh CHINTARAM are the founding leaders of VISIO Architecture Ltd (VISIO), bringing together decades of experience, design excellence, and strategic vision. Gaetan SIEW (now Honoray Chairman) is widely recognised in Mauritius and internationally for his long-standing contribution to the country’s architectural landscape, with a distinguished track record across major institutional and landmark projects. Vinesh CHINTARAM , the Managing Parner, represents a new-generation leadership, driving VISIO’s multidisciplinary growth with a strong focus on innovation, sustainability, and regional expansion. Vinesh is shapingd VISIO into a benchmark practice in Mauritius—combining legacy and forward-thinking ambition, and positioning the firm for increasing impact across Africa.

1981–2012

Founding stage and three decades of active engagement

"VISIO’s roots lie in Mauritius’ post-Independence nation-building era. The practice began as a partnership between architects W. Lampotang and G. Siew, contributing to a formative period of development marked by new towns, public institutions, and civic infrastructure—hospitals, offices, and major public facilities that supported a growing country.

Over time, the firm evolved into LSA Architects, building a strong reputation through diverse work across the island. During these three decades, LSA delivered a series of landmark projects that helped shape Mauritius’ modern built environment, including the Bank of Mauritius, Sir Seewoosagur Ramgoolam International Airport, the French Institute, multiple high-rise buildings in Port Louis, the Port Louis Theatre, and a broad range of residential projects—from affordable housing to high-end private developments. Beyond the projects themselves, this period established the firm’s foundations: professional rigour, contextual design thinking, and long-term engagement with the country’s evolving needs."

2013–2019

Refoundation and new leadership

"From 2013, the practice entered a new phase of renewal—strengthening its structure, modernising methods, and repositioning for growth. A renewed leadership framework combined G. Siew’s depth of experience with V. Chintaram’s dynamic and innovative drive, creating momentum for a more contemporary, multidisciplinary practice.

In 2016, VISIO Architecture Ltd was officially established as the continuity of LSA, formalising a new identity while carrying forward decades of expertise and credibility. With G. Siew as Chairman and V. Chintarama as CEO, the team grew significantly during this period, and VISIO consolidated its standing as a leading practice in the local market—recognised for clarity of thinking, design quality, and reliable delivery."

2020–2024

Building resilience amid the pandemic’s impact

"The year 2020 was initially meant to accelerate VISIO’s Africa strategy, but the pandemic disrupted markets, operations, and project timelines. VISIO’s ability to adapt was supported by preparation made earlier: an agile operating culture, cloud-based systems, and remote workflows that helped maintain continuity despite unprecedented constraints.

These years demanded rapid adjustment, stronger internal coordination, and a renewed focus on stability and delivery. Resilience became the central objective—sustaining project commitments, supporting the team, and reinforcing operational flexibility. The period also sharpened VISIO’s capacity to operate across multiple contexts, manage uncertainty, and remain dependable to clients even in volatile conditions."

2025

Transformative year

"In 2025, VISIO experienced a meaningful shift in its leadership landscape. G. Siew was appointed Ambassador of Mauritius to the People’s Republic of China and became Honorary Chairman of VISIO, while V. Chintaram continued as CEO, ensuring continuity of vision and strategic direction.

This year also marked a step toward expanding and strengthening leadership through the appointment of new Associates: G. Allet, M. Jeeloll, K. Mooroogen, and N. Joomum—a move that reflects institutional maturity and supports the firm’s next stage of growth."

Beyond 2025

Expanding VISIO’s African footprint

"VISIO’s ambition to position itself among Africa’s leading practices remains a priority. The firm brings a distinctive multidisciplinary track record, combining architecture with planning intelligence and delivery discipline. Its experience spans complex and niche sectors such as airports, high-rise developments, aquariums, complex industrial projects, urban regeneration, and mobility strategies, among others.

Looking ahead, VISIO aims to translate this depth of expertise into high-impact collaborations and projects across African cities and markets—delivering future-ready, context-driven solutions that create lasting value."

Integrity

"With a commitment to excellence and integrity, VISIO creates spaces that expand possibilities and deliver lasting value through thoughtful, context-driven architectural solutions."

Our mission is to design and deliver innovative, sustainable, and high-performing architectural, planning, and project management solutions that transform vision into reality. We collaborate closely with our clients—listening carefully, understanding priorities, and crafting spaces that inspire, function seamlessly, and endure. Through creative design, appropriate technology, and environmental stewardship, we aim to enhance the built environment, strengthen communities, and leave a lasting architectural legacy.

VISIO’s vision is to be a leading benchmark for architecture and planning in Mauritius, and to be recognised as one of the most significant multidisciplinary firms in Africa. We strive to shape modern environments through timeless, resilient design—where form and function work in harmony, communities are strengthened, and the natural context is respected. Guided by creativity, innovation, and disciplined delivery, we aim to elevate human experience and create meaningful connections between people and the places they inhabit.
