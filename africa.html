Why VISIO is Your Key Partner for Africa At VISIO, we go beyond architecture—we offer strategic solutions for Africa’s urban, territorial, and infrastructure transformation. We work with both Public and private partners and act as an enabler. With a legacy spanning over 40 years, initially under Gaetan SIEW’s leadership, VISIO has established itself as a leader in architecture, urban planning, and real estate development across Africa. Under the dynamic drive of Vinesh CHINTARAM over the past 15 years, VISIO has made significant advances in creativity, innovation, and ESG integration, positioning the firm as a future-ready partner for public and private sector clients alike.

Our team of visionary architects and designers brings ideas to life by blending creativity, sustainability, and modern technology. From conceptual design to project management, we are committed to delivering structures that exceed client expectations while harmonizing with their surroundings.

Unmatched African Reach
• Projects and engagements in over 30 African countries
• A professional network across more than 40 countries
• Proven experience in delivering complex assignments—ranging from consultancy to full architectural and urban design solutions
• A practice rooted in systematic collaboration with local architects and professionals, ensuring knowledge transfer, regulatory alignment, and contextual relevance
• Countries of Experience: Madagascar, Seychelles, Reunion Island, Mayotte, Algeria, Benin, Burkina Faso, Cameroon, Cote d’Ivoire, Djibouti, Equatorial Guinea, Ethiopia, Gabon, Ghana, Kenya, Liberia, Madagascar, Morocco, Mozambique, Nigeria, Congo, RD Congo, Reunion, Rwanda, Seychelles, Senegal, Sierra Leone, Sudan, South Africa, Togo, Tanzania, Tunisia, Uganda, Zambia.

Mauritius: Your Gateway to Africa Mauritius is not only VISIO’s home base—it is a true strategic springboard and launching pad for engaging with Africa. Through the Mauritius International Financial Centre (IFC), businesses and development partners benefit from:
• A robust legal framework
• Structured project financing mechanisms
• Tax-efficient vehicles for cross-border investment
• Stability and transparency as a gateway to African markets
This enables VISIO to offer end-to-end solutions, from early-stage feasibility and financing structuring to design, execution, and post-occupancy integration—seamlessly bridging vision and implementation.
